Welcome to the ReinObs mobile app! At ReinObs, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, disclosure, and protection of your personal information when you use our mobile app.

  1. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information from you when you use the ReinObs mobile app. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal Information: This is information that can be used to identify you, such as your name, email address, and phone number. We collect this information when you create an account with us, update your profile, or contact us for support.
  • Health Information: This is information about your health, such as the medications you take, the dosage, and the frequency of the medications. We collect this information when you enter it into the ReinObs mobile app or when you grant us permission to access this information from a third-party app or service.
  • Usage Information: This is information about how you use the ReinObs mobile app, such as the features you use, the time and date of your use, and any errors or crashes that occur. We collect this information to improve the performance and reliability of the ReinObs mobile app.
  • Device Information: This is information about the device you use to access the ReinObs mobile app, such as the device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers. We collect this information to personalize your experience and to improve the performance and reliability of the ReinObs mobile app.
  1. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect from you to provide you with the best possible experience when using the ReinObs mobile app. We may use your information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Improve Our Services: We use your information to provide you with the services you request and to improve the performance and functionality of the ReinObs mobile app.
  • To Personalize Your Experience: We use your information to personalize your experience with the ReinObs mobile app and to recommend medications based on your health information.
  • To Communicate With You: We use your information to communicate with you about the ReinObs mobile app, such as to provide you with updates or to respond to your inquiries or support requests.
  • To Comply With Legal Obligations: We may use your information to comply with legal obligations, such as responding to court orders or other legal requests.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with third parties in the following ways:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, data analysis, and customer service. These service providers are required to protect your information and use it only for the purposes for which it was shared.
  • Affiliates: We may share your information with our affiliates, who may use your information for their own purposes as described in their own privacy policies. We will only share your information with our affiliates if we have your consent to do so.
  • Business Transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will provide notice of such transfer on our website and will require the recipient to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or if we believe that such action is necessary to (a) comply with a legal obligation, (b) protect and defend our rights or property, (c) prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the ReinObs mobile app, (d) protect the personal safety of users of the ReinObs mobile app or the public, or (e) protect against legal liability.
